3 NIGHTS / 4 DAYS

TC01AT
COCHIN - MUNNAR – COCHIN

Day 1 – Munnar

Morning arrival at Cochin. On arrival, transfer to Hotel at Munnar. Rest of the day free. Overnight at Hotel.

Day 2 – Munnar

After breakfast ,morning sightseeing of Munnar. Rest of the day free for personal activities. Overnight at Hotel.

Day 3 – Cochin

After breakfast proceed to Cochin. Arrival at Cochin, rest of the day sightseeing of Cochin includes Jew synagogue, Dutch palace, Chinese fishing net etc. Rest of the day free. Overnight at Hotel.

Day 4 – Depart Cochin

After breakfast check out of the hotel and drive to Cochin Airport/Railway station.


TC01AT
COCHIN - MUNNAR – THEKKADY - COCHIN

DAY 1 – COCHIN – MUNNAR

Arrival at Kochi , and proceed to Munnar 145kms. Enroute visit Sankaracharya Temple , Aathakadu and Valera water falls, Karadippara View Point, Kallar Falls and Cheeyappara Falls . After lunch half day sightseeing. Visiting Rajamalai wild life, Naimakadu water falls. Overnight at Munnar.

DAY 2 – MUNNAR

After breakfast visit Madupetty Dam, Echo Point, Gundala Lake , Top Station, Photo Point, Indo-Swiss Project, Eravikulam Rajamalai National Park , Tea Museum , Pothamedu Sunset View, International Blossom Park etc. Evening free to relax. Overnight at Munnar.

DAY 3 – THEKKADY

After break fast transfer to Thekkady, 20 kms, 4 hour drives. Enjoy the scenic drive . On arrival check in the hotel. The most renowned place here is the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. You will spot the elephant, tiger, bison, spotted deer, samba deer, gaur etc. The sanctuary is open all round the year. In the evening, Game viewing by motorized boat at Periyar Lake can be organized (not included). Overnight stay at the hotel.

DAY 4 – DEPART COCHIN

After breakfast departure for Thekkey to Kochi 200kms, 5hrs. Afternoon sight seeing. visiting Jewish Synagogue, Dutch Palace , St.Thomas Church , St.Mary's cathedral, Chinese Fish Nets and Bolghaty Palace . Drop at Kochi Airport/Rly station.
