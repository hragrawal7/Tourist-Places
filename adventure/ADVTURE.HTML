Adventure Escapades

India promises every adventure lover an active and healthy holidays while learning new skills and reaching new heights. Vast open spaces of India, wilderness areas, towering mountains, vast coastal and desert regions makes it an ideal destination for enjoying astounding variety of adventure sports and activities.

With high rise mountains in the north and the Arabian Sea and the Indian Ocean on the south, India provides an excellent opportunity for a number of adventure tours. You can take an adventure tour to the snow capped peaks in the Himalays or raft down the gushing rivers in the foothills of the Himalayas.
