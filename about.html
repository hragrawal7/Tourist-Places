TRAVEL TIPS

VISA

A valid Visa and Passport is necessary for the foreigner travellers to India.

TRAVEL BY TRAINS

Trains, in India are the best way to get around. Train travel is cheap, reasonably comfortable, and still fairly safe. Night trains offer an excellent way of moving between cities while saving on hotel costs.

TRAVEL BY TAXI’S

Airports and railway stations have pre-paid taxi counters operated by the police. Tourists should avail their services as the government fixes thei r fares. Public buses operating within city limits could be avoided.

HEALTH CHECK

If you are travelling to India during the peak summer season, direct exposure to sunlight should be avoided to minimize chances of heat strokes. Roadside food should be avoided as chances of diseases like jaundice are always present. Indian food being spicy and oily, large quantities should never be consumed even while eating at a good eatery.
